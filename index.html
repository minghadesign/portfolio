Funding Application Workflow: Lingnan Education Organization

1.Application Submission (Applicant)
The applicant navigates to the funding application website. They complete and submit the required application form.


2.First-Level Review (University Professor)
A university professor receives and reviews the submitted application. The professor is also responsible for periodically monitoring the progress reports related to the funded project.


3.Final Verification (Lingnan Education Organization)**
The application, having been reviewed by the professor, is forwarded to the Lingnan Education Organization. The organization performs the final check and verification to complete the approval process.


HKSKH Welfare Council Elderly Home Family Portal

1. Core Objectives
For Families:
To provide a convenient, self-service platform for families to stay informed about their loved one's care and administrative matters.
For the Nursing Home:
To increase operational efficiency by reducing the time staff spend on handling routine inquiries.


2. User Workflow for Family Members
Step 1: Financial Management
View Monthly Bills: Families can log in to view detailed monthly invoices for accommodation and other services.
Action: This allows them to track expenses and manage payments without needing to call or wait for paper statements.

Step 2: Meal Plan Customization
Order Monthly Menus: The portal displays the meal options for the upcoming month.
Action: Families can review and select or customize the meal plan for their relative directly through the website.

Step 3: Daily Health & Care Monitoring
Check Daily Vitals: Access to daily records of the resident's blood pressure readings.
Review Medication Schedule: View the specific times and types of medication administered each day.
View Therapy Appointments: Check the schedule for physical therapy sessions and other treatments.
Action: This provides families with near real-time updates on their relative's health and well-being, offering peace of mind.


3. Business Impact (Efficiency Gain)
By empowering families with direct access to information, the portal significantly reduces the volume of incoming calls and emails to the nursing home's customer service staff.
This frees up administrative and care staff to focus on more direct, high-value tasks related to resident care.


AR Interactive Game for Kaohsiung Public Library
Client: Kaohsiung Public Library
Developer: Hamastar Technology Co. Ltd.


1. Project Objective
To introduce children under 12 to Augmented Reality through a creative and collaborative "draw-to-life" experience.
To align with the government's initiative to promote early engagement with new technologies.


2. My Role & Responsibilities
Storyboard Artist:
I visualized the game's narrative and user flow, creating scene-by-scene illustrations to guide the development team.
UI/UX Designer:
I designed the user interface (UI) and the overall user experience (UX), ensuring the app was intuitive, engaging, and easy for young children to navigate.
3D Modeler:
I created the 3D models for the characters and game assets.
Animator:
I brought the 3D characters and elements to life with animations, making the game dynamic and visually appealing.


3. User Experience Workflow
Physical Creation:
Each child draws and colors their own animal character on a piece of paper.
AR Scanning & Transformation:
The child uses a mobile device to scan their drawing. The application's AR technology converts the 2D drawing into a unique 3D character.
Game Immersion:
The newly created 3D character appears in the mobile game, allowing the child to see their personal creation come to life.
Collaborative Gameplay:
Children use their characters to participate in a story-based game that requires teamwork and cooperation to progress.

KOL Marketing Campaign for EVRbeauty

To attract female customers with significant spending power to purchase EVRbeauty's services or products, likely as gifts for their male partners, friends, or family members.

Target Audience
Women with medium to high disposable income who value quality, luxury, and grooming for both themselves and their partners.

Marketing Strategy: Influencer Collaboration
Influencer Profile: The campaign will focus on identifying and partnering with a large number of Key Opinion Leaders (KOLs) who fit the "貴婦" (gweifuh / affluent lady) archetype. These influencers typically showcase:
A luxury lifestyle (fine dining, high fashion, exclusive events).
An appreciation for high-end beauty and wellness treatments.
A sophisticated and trusted persona.


Authentic Testimonial Campaign for EVRbeauty

To build trust and credibility by showcasing real, dramatic results from actual customers.

Target Audience
Individuals (both men and women) who are actively seeking solutions for persistent and difficult skin problems and are often skeptical of traditional advertising.

Marketing Strategy: User-Generated "Before & After" Content
Content Focus: The campaign will center on creating a large volume of genuine "before and after" case studies.
Participant Profile: Instead of using professional models or KOLs, the campaign will recruit "素人" everyday people who have genuine skin issues.
Specific Problems to Highlight:
Pitted Acne Scars (凹凸洞): Showcasing improvement in skin texture from severe acne.
Hormonal Melasma (荷爾蒙斑): Demonstrating the reduction of difficult-to-treat hyperpigmentation.
